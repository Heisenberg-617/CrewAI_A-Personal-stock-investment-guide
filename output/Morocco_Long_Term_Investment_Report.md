## Executive Summary
This report provides a comprehensive analysis of the Moroccan economy and identifies the top sectors for long-term investment. ## Morocco Macroeconomic Outlook
Morocco has a diverse economy with a strong agricultural sector, a growing manufacturing sector, and a large services sector. The country has a strategic location, a stable political environment, and a favorable business climate. However, Morocco faces challenges such as a high unemployment rate, a large trade deficit, and a dependence on imports.
